Organization: UNC Neurosurgery
Topic: Neurosurgery Resident Education
Objective: Radiology and Pathology flashcard like learning
Audience: Neurosurgery residents
Background: Neurosurgery residents need mental breaks when studying to retain information. Often they feel guilty when doing intense study to take the necessary breaks for retention. The game would provide enough humor and mental downtime so that information can be retained and meaningful.

Short Description of the Game

The game will be an educational game that has a clear delineation between its educational and entertainment aspects. The game’s educational aspect will be extremely dry while the entertainment aspect will be extremely humorous.

Current Stage

Initial setup for game development. The game will be web based. We will be using Github as source control and we are hoping to utilize Phaser.io as the game framework. The languages we will be using are Javascript, HTML5 and CSS3.

Ideas

The president has assembled a special taskforce to avert the free world from disaster

  • One of the levels could be that Honey Boo Boo has reached critical mass and unless she can lose some weight she is going to become a black hole and consume the earth.
  • Cat videos have grown in popularity to the point that they take up over 90% of data space on servers and all skilled workers in society spend their time trying to create better cat videos. This has resulted in civilization coming to a standstill. The taskforce’s mission would be to somehow eliminate cat videos or their popularity.
  • Taylor Swift’s music turns out to be a sinister plot to turn the masses into a zombie army. The taskforce’s mission is to dodge the zombies and reach the loudspeakers to shut off the loudspeakers.
